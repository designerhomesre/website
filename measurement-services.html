What's Included

Everything you need for an accurate, defensible listing — delivered fast.

ANSI-Standard Measurements

Every measurement follows ANSI Z765 standards — the industry benchmark for residential square footage. Your listing data will be accurate, defensible, and compliant.

Licensed Appraiser

All measurements are completed by a state-certified residential appraiser — not an unlicensed technician. Your clients and their lenders can trust the results.

Floor Plan with GLA

Receive a professional floor plan showing gross living area (GLA) and individually labeled rooms — ready for your listing, marketing materials, or MLS entry.

24–48 Hour Turnaround

From inspection to delivery in 24–48 hours. We know listings move fast — your measurement report won't hold you up.

A simple, streamlined process from request to delivery.
