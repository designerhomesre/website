What is Mass Appraisal?

Mass appraisal is the systematic valuation of groups of properties as of a given date using standardized methods, statistical testing, and common data. This approach enables efficient, consistent, and defensible valuations at scale.

Designer Homes provides expert consulting to government agencies and tax offices throughout North Carolina. Our services help jurisdictions establish reliable property assessment systems, conduct reappraisals, defend valuations in appeals, and maintain compliance with professional standards.
