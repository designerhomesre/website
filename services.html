Appraisal Services

Expert valuations for every scenario — accurate, timely, and professionally delivered.

At Designer Homes Real Estate Services, we understand that no two appraisal assignments are exactly alike. Whether you need a straightforward residential valuation, a specialized assessment for a complex scenario, or a high-stakes appraisal that will withstand scrutiny, our team brings professional expertise, local market knowledge, and rigorous methodology to every project.

Our State Certified appraiser combines deep credentials in both private and public sector valuation, ensuring your assignment receives the appropriate expertise and attention it deserves. From divorce settlements and estate planning to new construction and government consulting, we deliver valuations you can trust.

Our Valuation Services

🏘️ Private Appraisals

Independent property valuations for purchases, refinances, financial planning, and personal decision-making. Our private appraisals provide accurate market value opinions backed by thorough research and local market expertise.

Ideal for: Homeowners, buyers, sellers, financial planners
