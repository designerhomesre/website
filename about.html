About Designer Homes Real Estate Services

Professional expertise meets modern technology — delivering accurate valuations and premier service to the Triangle region.

Professional Photo

Keith Manning Jr.

Principal, Designer Homes Real Estate Services

Keith Manning Jr. is the Principal of Designer Homes Real Estate Services, a full-service appraisal and valuation firm serving the Triangle region of North Carolina. With credentials spanning both private and public sector valuation, Keith brings a rare combination of expertise to every assignment.

As a State Certified Residential Appraiser, Licensed Real Estate Broker with Pinnacle Realty Group, and Real Property Appraiser I certified by NCDOR, Keith serves clients across a wide spectrum of valuation needs — from private appraisals for attorneys and homeowners to mass appraisal consulting for government agencies.

His role as Staff Appraiser with Floyd Wicker & Associates further deepens his experience in complex valuation scenarios, tax assessment, and reappraisal work.
